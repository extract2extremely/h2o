## 🖨️ How to Use

### View Report
1. Open Savings Details
2. Click "Report" button
3. See beautiful preview

### Print Report
1. Click "Print Statement"
2. Browser print dialog opens
3. Choose printer or "Save as PDF"

### Download PDF
1. Click "Download PDF" button
2. Professional PDF file downloads
3. Ready to share or archive
