# 🎯 Savings Report - Quick Start Guide

## How to Generate a Savings Account Report

### Step 1: Open Savings Details
1. Go to **Savings** section from the sidebar
2. Click on any **savings account** to view details
3. You'll see the account holder information and schedule

### Step 2: Click Report Button
- Look for the **"Report"** button with a **PDF icon** 🔴📋
- It's located next to the "Edit" button at the top right
- **Click the Report button**

### Step 3: Professional Report Opens
A beautiful professional report will display showing:
- ✅ Account holder information
- ✅ Savings goal and progress
- ✅ Collection history
- ✅ Installment details
- ✅ Staff signatures

### Step 4: Choose Your Action

#### To Print the Report:
1. Click **"Print Statement"** button (purple button)
2. Browser's print dialog opens
3. Select your printer
4. Click **Print** to print on paper or "Save as PDF"

#### To Download as PDF:
1. Click **"Download PDF"** button (green button)
2. File automatically saves as `Savings_Report_[Name].pdf`
3. File appears in your Downloads folder
4. You can email, share, or archive it

#### To Close:
1. Click the **"X"** button or **"Close"** button
2. Or click outside the report window
3. Returns to Savings Details view

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Where is the Report button?
**A:** It's at the top right of the Savings Details view, next to the "Edit" button. Look for the red PDF icon.

### Q: Can I print directly?
**A:** Yes! Click "Print Statement" button in the report to print directly.

### Q: Where does the PDF save?
**A:** To your browser's default Downloads folder (usually `C:\Users\[YourName]\Downloads`).

### Q: What format is the PDF?
**A:** Professional A4 format suitable for printing and archival.

### Q: Can I share the PDF?
**A:** Yes! You can email it or share via messaging apps.

### Q: Is the data secure?
**A:** Yes! Reports are generated locally on your device, not uploaded anywhere.

### Q: Can I customize the report?
**A:** The system admin can customize styling and content in the code.

### Q: What if something doesn't print right?
**A:** Try a different browser, adjust zoom (Ctrl+0), or check print preview.

### Q: Can I schedule reports automatically?
**A:** Not yet, but you can generate them whenever needed.

### Q: What if the button doesn't work?
**A:** Make sure the app is running via HTTP server (not file://), not via the file:// protocol.
